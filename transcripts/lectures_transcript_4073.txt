Intersectional studies provide rich data, but cross-sectional studies may be more practical. Use the IF-THEN framework to build hypotheses. Researchers must edit data, handle missing data, and categorize all variables. Use hypotheses to test group differences or to establish a network of associations. Hypotheses should be made a priori, usually at the 0.05 alpha level. Minimize number of hypotheses to reduce error. And communicate results in an appropriate manner, depending on your audience.
